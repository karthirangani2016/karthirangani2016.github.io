What is the difference between symmetric and asymmetric encryption?
Symmetric encryption uses a single private key for both encryption and decryption, whereas asymmetric encryption uses a public key for encryption and a separate private key for decryption.
What is the principle of "Least Privilege"?
It is the security practice of limiting user access rights and permissions to the bare minimum required to perform their specific job functions.
How does a VPN protect a user's data?
A VPN creates an encrypted tunnel for data transmission, masking the user's IP address and securing their traffic from local network eavesdroppers.
