Where is the Chennai Lab?
The Chennai Lab is located in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, India. It is the new base of operations for JARVIS 5.0 after moving from Tirupati.
Who is the Lead Developer of JARVIS?
The Lead Developer is Karthikeya, a brilliant 10-year-old engineer who built the Neural Brain.
What is the Tronica Subwoofer?
The Tronica Subwoofer is the official audio output device for JARVIS, connected via the AUX port for high-fidelity voice responses.
What is a Neural Brain?
A Neural Brain is a type of AI called an MLPClassifier that learns patterns from data to think for itself instead of just following hardcoded rules.
How did the Lab move to Chennai?
The Lab moved from Tirupati to Chennai in June 2026, marking a new chapter in JARVIS's development.
What is Chennai famous for?
Chennai is known as the Gateway to South India and is famous for its beaches, temples, and being a major hub for technology and engineering.
What is the fastest animal?
The fastest land animal is the Cheetah, but the fastest bird is the Peregrine Falcon, which can dive at over 300 km/h.
What is a Black Hole?
A black hole is a region of spacetime where gravity is so strong that nothing, including light, can escape from it.
What is the Raspberry Pi?
The Raspberry Pi is a tiny, powerful credit-card sized computer that acts as the physical body for JARVIS.
What is Minecraft?
Minecraft is a sandbox game where you can build anything you can imagine out of blocks, much like how you build JARVIS with code.
