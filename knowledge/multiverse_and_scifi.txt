What is the Multiverse Theory?
The Multiverse is a hypothetical group of multiple universes. Together, these universes comprise everything that exists: the entirety of space, time, matter, energy, information, and the physical laws and constants that describe them. The different universes within the multiverse are called "parallel universes."
What is a Dimension?
In physics and mathematics, a dimension is a direction in space. We live in three spatial dimensions (length, width, height) and one temporal dimension (time). Theoretical physics, like String Theory, suggests there could be as many as 11 dimensions.
What is Interdimensional Travel?
Interdimensional travel is a staple of science fiction that involves moving between different dimensions or parallel universes. In theoretical physics, this is often discussed in the context of wormholes or the "Many-Worlds" interpretation of quantum mechanics.
What is 'Stranger Things'?
Stranger Things is a popular sci-fi horror series set in the 1980s. It follows a group of friends who encounter supernatural forces and secret government exploits, leading them to discover a parallel dimension known as the Upside Down.
What is the Upside Down?
The Upside Down is a dark, cold, and toxic mirror dimension of our own world. It is inhabited by terrifying creatures like the Demogorgon and the Mind Flayer, and it is linked to our world through a "Gate" opened by psychokinetic experiments.
Who is Eleven from Stranger Things?
Eleven is a young girl with powerful psychokinetic and telepathic abilities. She was a test subject in a secret government program (Project MKUltra) and was the one who accidentally opened the gate to the Upside Down while exploring the astral plane.
What is String Theory?
String Theory is a theoretical framework in which the point-like particles of particle physics are replaced by one-dimensional objects called strings. It requires extra spatial dimensions beyond the four we experience to be mathematically consistent.
What is the Many-Worlds Interpretation?
The Many-Worlds Interpretation (MWI) is a theory in quantum mechanics which implies that all possible alternate histories and futures are real, each representing an actual "world" or universe. Every time a quantum event occurs, the universe splits into multiple branches.
What is a Wormhole?
A wormhole, also known as an Einstein-Rosen bridge, is a theoretical passage through space-time that could create shortcuts for long journeys across the universe or even provide a path between different universes.
What are the 'Demogorgons'?
Demogorgons are predatory creatures originating from the Upside Down. They are humanoid but have no visible eyes and a mouth that opens like a flower. They are highly resilient and hunt by tracking the scent of blood across dimensions.
